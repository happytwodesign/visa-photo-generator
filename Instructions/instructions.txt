Web service for Schengen visa photo generation based on schengen requirements
User flow:
User uploads photo.
User clicks Generate.
Backend operations:
Resize photo to 35mm x 45mm.
Remove background from photo.
Change background to light gray.
Fit the face to meet the head height requirement.
Fix head tilt.
Adjust image contrast.
Review the image meets photo requirements and highlight the ones that are not met and the ones that are met.
Download options:
User downloads a single photo for internet submission.
User downloads a single photo and templates with resulted photo for printing in different paper formats (A4, A5, A6).
